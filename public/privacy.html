Privacy

Privacy information for the Blue Wing Labs website.

Last updated April 14, 2026.

Blue Wing Labs keeps this website simple. The website does not include a user account system, checkout flow, or embedded analytics dashboard.

What the website collects

The website itself does not ask visitors to submit personal information through an on-page form. If you contact Blue Wing Labs by email, your email address and message content may be used to reply to you, share access information, or send product updates you requested.

How contact information is used

Blue Wing Labs may use email communications to:

  • reply to support or product questions,
  • share access or release updates you requested,
  • follow up about Blue Wing Labs availability.

Third-party services

When you email Blue Wing Labs, your message is handled through your email provider and the Blue Wing Labs email provider. Their services are governed by their own privacy terms.

Blue Wing Labs iOS app

This section applies specifically to the Blue Wing Labs iOS app and explains what information may be stored in the app, how it is used, and how you can remove it.

Data we collect and store in the app

The Blue Wing Labs iOS app may store:

  • local inventory information,
  • shopping list entries,
  • tie history and progress,
  • app preferences and settings,
  • optional submission details and photos you choose to provide,
  • optional backup data synced through Apple CloudKit if you enable it.

How we collect app data

App data is collected or created in the following ways:

  • entered directly by you in the app,
  • selected by you from the Photos library when you choose to attach images,
  • generated from your app usage and saved app state,
  • synced through Apple CloudKit when CloudKit backup is enabled.

How we use app data

Blue Wing Labs uses app data to:

  • provide core app functionality such as inventory, lists, and progress tracking,
  • support backup and restore when CloudKit syncing is enabled,
  • review and respond to optional submissions or support requests you send.

Third parties used by the app

The iOS app may interact with the following third parties:

  • Apple CloudKit and iCloud, only if you enable backup or sync features,
  • your mail app and email provider, if you choose to contact support or send a submission,
  • Trident, Orvis, or YouTube only when you choose to open external links to those services.

Blue Wing Labs does not send your data to Trident, Orvis, or YouTube unless you intentionally open one of those external links from the app or website.

Retention and deletion

Data stored locally in the app remains on your device until you clear it in the app, remove the app, restore different data, or otherwise replace it. If CloudKit backup is enabled, synced data may remain in your iCloud account until you turn off backup, delete the backup or app data from iCloud, replace it with newer synced data, or request help deleting it where applicable.

How to revoke access or delete app data

You can manage or delete app data by:

  • resetting inventory, lists, or progress inside the app, if those controls are available,
  • stopping use of CloudKit backup or removing app data from your iCloud storage,
  • contacting Blue Wing Labs for deletion help if you need assistance.

Tracking and analytics

Blue Wing Labs does not use third-party advertising trackers in the website or iOS app. Blue Wing Labs also does not use analytics in the iOS app unless this policy is updated to say otherwise.

Questions

For privacy questions, contact support@bluewinglabs.app.
