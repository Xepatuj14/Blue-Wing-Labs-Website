Support

Support for the Blue Wing Labs iOS app.

Last updated May 4, 2026.

Blue Wing Labs support is handled directly by email. If you need help with the iOS app, App Store installation, privacy questions, data deletion, a problem you ran into while using the product, general feedback, or a feature request, contact Clarkii Outdoors and include a short description of the issue.

Blue Wing Labs is a product operated by Clarkii Outdoors LLC, and this support page is provided for Blue Wing Labs users by Clarkii Outdoors LLC.

What to include in your message

  • the device model and iOS version, if you are reporting an app issue,
  • what you were trying to do when the problem happened,
  • any screenshots or photos you want to share, if helpful,
  • whether your question is about support, privacy, access, or a submission.

What Blue Wing Labs can help with

  • questions about App Store installation or app availability,
  • help with app behavior, saved data, or backup questions,
  • privacy or deletion requests,
  • requests to stop product update emails,
  • general feedback and feature requests,
  • questions about optional submissions sent to Blue Wing Labs.
